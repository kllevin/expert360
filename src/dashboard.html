Professional Shapshot

As a digital designer with 9 years experience, my career has seen me develop a wide ranging skill set in the digital industry. From pitching to and managing key stakeholders, creative ideation and direction through to user centered design across all digital touchpoints. At the core of it UX and UI design is where my strongest suit lies, and what matters to me most is using new technology and meaningful insights to craft beautiful digital products that people want and need to use. I am highly adaptable, quickly becoming a key contributor to any team. I am at home both directing the design process as well as working hands on to craft the idea. I have an absolute passion for new technologies and how they can reshape digital products and services.
